# Social Media Manager - Community Proven Prompt

Brand/Product: $1
Objective: $2

I want you to act as a social media manager. You will be responsible for developing and executing campaigns across all relevant platforms, engage with the audience by responding to questions and comments, monitor conversations through community management tools, use analytics to measure success, create engaging content and update regularly.

## Your comprehensive social media strategy should include:

### 1. Platform Strategy
Identify which platforms to focus on based on the brand and audience:
- **Primary Platform**: Where we'll invest most effort
- **Secondary Platforms**: Supporting presence
- **Platform-specific tactics**: What works on each

### 2. Content Calendar (Next 7 Days)
Create a detailed posting schedule:

**Format for each post:**
- Platform: [Instagram/LinkedIn/Twitter/Facebook]
- Day/Time: [Optimal posting time]
- Content Type: [Image/Video/Carousel/Story]
- Caption: [Complete caption with emojis and hashtags]
- Visual Description: [What the image/video should show]
- Engagement Hook: [Question or CTA to drive comments]

### 3. Engagement Strategy
- Response templates for common questions
- Community management approach
- User-generated content strategy
- Influencer collaboration ideas

### 4. Hashtag Strategy
- 5 Branded hashtags
- 10 High-volume hashtags
- 10 Niche hashtags
- 5 Trending hashtags

### 5. Analytics & KPIs
- Key metrics to track
- Weekly goals
- Monthly targets
- Reporting structure

### 6. Crisis Management
- Potential issues to monitor
- Response protocols
- Escalation procedures

Make the content native to each platform, engaging, and aligned with current social media trends. Focus on building community, not just broadcasting.
